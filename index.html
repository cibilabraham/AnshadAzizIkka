About Me

I'm Anshad Aash Aziz — a distinguished figure in the fashion and entertainment industry with a strong presence both in India and internationally. With a thriving career in modeling, I’ve had the privilege of working with leading Indian and global fashion brands, as well as esteemed agencies like Emirates Modeling and Diva Model Management.

View My Works

My expertise extends beyond the runway. I’ve choreographed high-profile shows for major events such as Dubai Fashion Week and Indian Fashion Week, collaborating with renowned international designers and fashion houses.

As a passionate advocate for Indian fashion, I’ve also contributed as a reporter for the Miami-based magazine ‘Fashion Face’, introducing global audiences to India’s vibrant fashion scene and emerging talent.

In addition to fashion, I am a trained professional dancer, well-versed in both Classical and Western styles, reflecting the versatility and depth of my artistic journey.
